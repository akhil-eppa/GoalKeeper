Frequently Asked Questions
The process is very easy.
All you need to do is login, go to the Goals and Habits Page and set your goals and timeframe needed to complete them.
Currently there is only a common basic plan available for all users at no cost.
Hence there are no upgrades available as of now.
One needs to set as to how often he would like to repeat his/her habits.
Based on this all the necessary tracking will take place and the user would be reminded at regular intervals.
As one keeps completing tasks(goals and habits), he/she gets medals. The medals are awarded as follows:
  • 5 Tasks - Bronze Medal
  • 10 Tasks - Silver Medal
  • 20 Tasks - Gold Medal
GoalKeeper offers you the platform to assimilate all your habits and goals that you wish to inculcate in your
day-to-day life. It also provides incentives on accomplishing tasks in the form of medals to keep you
motivated enough to complete them.
We send reminders incase you opt for them. We also provide you with a statistics and analytics page to
give you the numerical nitty-gritty of your tasks.
