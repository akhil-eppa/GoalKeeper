About Us

About GoalKeeper


GoalKeeper was founded to address a growing problem related to the decrease in attention span, due to which we often fall short of achieving the goals and targets we set for ourselves.

Our purpose is to allow everyone to improve themselves by setting time-bound goals and habits and help them to go about achieving them.

Our Story


How does one usually set goals? By remembering them by the back of their heads? By writing them on a piece of paper that they may not refer to again? Then forgetting about all this and hence forget the goals that one had set for themselves?

Setting goals and pursuing them with a one hundred percent effort while keeping track of them has always been a difficult affair.

Then came GoalKeeper.

What do we do?


GoalKeeper offers features and services that have helped people focus more on achieving their goals. GoalKeeper will change how everyone achieves their goals.

For every goal set, there will be regular follow-ups and reminders, thus always reminding you of what you set out to achieve. What more? Get medals as you go about accomplishing one goal after another.
